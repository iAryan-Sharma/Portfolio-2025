My Project
Workflow

1.

Client Meeting & Requirement Gathering

Understanding the client's vision is crucial — I kick things off with a detailed discussion to align goals, set expectations, and read minds (well, almost).

2.

Design Concept & Approval

Based on our chat, I craft design mockups or wireframes — basically blueprints that look just good enough to get excited, and bad enough to invite feedback.

3.

Development & Coding

Once the design’s a go, I dive into code — building pixel-perfect components that look great on every screen (even that ancient iPhone your client won’t give up).

4.

Testing & Quality Assurance

Before launch, I test across browsers and devices — yes, even the ones that think JavaScript is a suggestion. Bugs? Not on my watch.

5.

Deployment & Post-launch Support

After going live, I don’t disappear — I monitor, support, and make fixes faster than you can say “Can we change just one thing?”
