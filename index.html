My Project
Workflow

1.

Client Meeting & Requirement Gathering

Understanding the client's vision is crucial. I begin with a detailed discussion to gather requirements, clarify goals, and set expectations for the project.

2.

Design Concept & Approval

Based on the initial discussion, I create design mockups or wireframes. These are then shared with the client for feedback and approval, ensuring the design aligns with their vision.

3.

Development & Coding

Once the design is approved, I start coding the front-end components. I focus on writing clean, efficient code, ensuring responsiveness and compatibility across devices.

4.

Testing & Quality Assurance

Before launch, I thoroughly test the website across different browsers and devices. I also check for any bugs or issues to ensure a smooth user experience.

5.

Deployment & Post-launch Support

After testing, the project is deployed to the live server. I continue to monitor the website post-launch, providing support and making any necessary updates or fixes.
